Before confirming any cleanup action, you should:

  • Ensure you have backups of important photos (we recommend using iCloud or another backup service)

3.2 Understanding Deletion

  • Deleted photos are moved to the "Recently Deleted" album in iOS Photos
  • Photos in "Recently Deleted" are permanently removed after 30 days by iOS
  • You can restore photos from "Recently Deleted" within the 30-day window
  • After 30 days, recovery may not be possible
